About

Hello, my name is Yoojin Kim, and also Lucia.

I am a Master student at Music and Audio Computing Lab, Graduate School of Culture Technology, KAIST. Before joining the lab, I received a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Yonsei University, and minor was Child and Family Studies.

I'm intereseted in research topics about Data Analytics, Music Information Retrieval, and their intersections with various aspects of human life.

Currently I'm working intensively on Emotional Symbolic Music Data Analysis, and I mostly use Machine Learning and Deep Learning for my research. For a full history of my career, you can refer to my CV(last update in Oct 2020) .
